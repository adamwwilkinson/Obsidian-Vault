### The Suffix -nya
The lazy way to form a noun.

Tinggi - tall
TIngginya - the height

### The Prefix -pe
The do-er of an action.
bermain - to play
permain - player

### The Suffix -an
A concrete noun, the stuff that the base word does.
makan - to eat
makanan - food

### Pe- -an
A abstract nouns, concepts rather than tangible things.
Belarjar- to study
Pelarjaran - lesson

### Ke- -an
Most nouns are ke- -an nouns.

### Relative Position, Velocity, and Acceleration
The position of $B$ relative to $A$ is $r_{BA} = r_B - r_A$.
The velocity of $B$ relative to $A$ is $v_{BA} = v_B - v_A$.
The acceleration of $B$ relative to $A$ is $a_{BA} = a_B - a_A$.

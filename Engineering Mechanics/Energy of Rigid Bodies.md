### Kinetic Energy
$$T = \frac 1 2 m (v_G)^2 + \frac 1 2 I_G \omega^2$$

#### Pure Translation
$$T = \frac 1 2 m (v_G)^2$$

#### Pure Rotation
$$T = 0.5 I_O \omega^2$$

### Work of a Couple
$$U_M = \int_{\theta_1}^{\theta_2} M \mathrm d \theta$$

If the couple moment is constant,
$$U_M = M(\theta_2 - \theta_1)$$

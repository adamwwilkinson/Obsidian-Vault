### Horizontal Motion
Since $a_x = 0$, the velocity of the horizontal motion remains constant. The position in the x direction can be determined by:
$$x = x_o + (v_{ox})t$$

### Vertical Motion
$a_y = -g$
$v_y = v_{oy} - gt$
$y = y_o + (v_{oy})t - 0.5gt^2$
$v_y^2 = v_{oy}^2 - 2g(y - y_o)$
